Upgrade in Progress

The wizard displays two progress bars to illustrate the status of the upgrade. The top bar shows the fraction of the complete upgrade procedure that has been completed. The lower bar shows the fraction of the current operation that has been completed.

Wait for the upgrade to complete.

If you wish to stop the upgrade then click on the Abort button. However, this should only be used as a last resort – it may leave the BlueCore module in a non-operational state.
